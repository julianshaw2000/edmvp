What style of landing page?

Pick the approach that fits your target audience (B2B compliance buyers)

A

Minimal SaaS

Clean hero with headline, subtitle, CTA button, then 3-4 feature cards. Simple pricing section. No fancy animations. Think Linear, Stripe, Vercel — professional, fast-loading, lets the product speak.

B

Product-Led

Hero with a screenshot/mockup of the actual dashboard. "See it in action" approach. Feature sections with inline product images. Social proof section. More content, more scrolling, more persuasive.

C

Compliance-Focused

Lead with the problem: "Manual compliance audits cost weeks." Industry-specific language (RMAP, OECD, 3TG). Trust badges, certification logos. Targets compliance officers who need to justify the purchase internally.
