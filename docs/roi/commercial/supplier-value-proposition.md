# Auditraks Supplier Value Proposition

---

## The Problem for Suppliers

Compliance is usually something done *to* suppliers, not *for* them. You fill in questionnaires, upload certificates, respond to audits — and get nothing back. The buyer benefits. You just do the work.

## What Auditraks Gives You

### 1. Material Passport — Yours to Share
Every completed batch generates a verified Material Passport. It proves your minerals were responsibly sourced, with tamper-evident SHA-256 hash chains that anyone can verify.

**You own it. Share it with any customer, bank, or auditor — not just the buyer who asked for it.**

### 2. Fewer Audit Requests
Your buyers verify compliance through the platform instead of emailing you for documents. One set of records serves every buyer relationship.

### 3. Compliance Dashboard
See your compliance track record at a glance: how many batches passed, which ones were flagged, and why. No more guessing whether your next audit will be a problem.

### 4. Public Verification
Every Material Passport has a QR code. Scan it and verify the batch — no login required. Your customers' customers can verify too.

### 5. Exportable Compliance Summary
Download your compliance history for annual reports, bank applications, or ESG disclosures.

## How It Works

1. **Your buyer invites you** — you get an email, create your account (free)
2. **Log custody events** — extraction, assay, concentration, trading, smelting, export
3. **Compliance runs automatically** — 5 checks per batch, instant results
4. **Download your Material Passport** — share the verification link with anyone

**Cost to you: $0.** Supplier accounts are always free.

### auditraks — For Suppliers

**What is it?** A platform where you log your mineral custody events (mining, assay, processing, trading, export) and get a verified Material Passport in return.

**What does it cost?** Nothing. Supplier accounts are free.

**What do you get?**
- Verified Material Passport for every completed batch
- Shareable proof of responsible sourcing (QR code verification)
- Compliance dashboard showing your track record
- Fewer audit requests from buyers

**What do you do?**
1. Accept the invitation email from your buyer
2. Create your account (email + password, 2 minutes)
3. Log custody events as your batches move through the supply chain
4. Download Material Passports when batches are complete

**How long does it take?** Logging a custody event takes 2–3 minutes. A typical batch has 4–6 events over its lifecycle.

**Who sees your data?** Only your buyer and their compliance team. Your data is never shared with other buyers or made public without your permission. Material Passports are shared only when you choose to share them.
