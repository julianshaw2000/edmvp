## Trial Terms

- **Duration:** 60 days
- **Credit card required:** No
- **Tier:** Full Pro features during trial
- **Conversion:** Auto-downgrades to Starter if no payment method added
- **Data retention:** All data preserved on conversion

## Billing Terms

- Monthly billing by default, charged on subscription anniversary date
- Annual billing available at 2-months-free discount
- All payments processed via Stripe
- Invoices available in Stripe customer portal
- Currency: USD. Tax (VAT/GST) added where applicable based on buyer location.

## Overage Handling (Starter Only)

- Approaching batch limit: warning at 40/50 batches
- At 50 batches: cannot create new batches, existing data unaffected
- Resolution: upgrade to Pro or archive completed batches

## Cancellation Policy

- Cancel anytime from the billing settings page
- Access continues until end of current billing period
- No refunds for partial periods
- Data export available for 90 days after cancellation
- Data deleted 90 days after cancellation

## Enterprise Engagement

Enterprise pricing is custom-quoted based on:
- Number of supply chain tiers tracked
- Compliance framework requirements
- Integration requirements (ERP, existing CMRT tools)
- Volume of annual batches
- Support SLA requirements

Minimum engagement: $999/mo or $9,990/yr.
